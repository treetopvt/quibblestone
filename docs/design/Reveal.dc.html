The Wobbly Wizard & the Golden Sock

Long ago, a wobbly wizard named Bartholomew set out to find the legendary Golden Sock. He galloped across a squishy swamp, where a gigantic frog dared him to yodel at the moon.

“By my sparkly beard!” Bartholomew cried — and the whole kingdom laughed for nine days straight. The end.
